Research and Methodology

Qualitative Research

  • 10 semi-structured interviews with amateur athletes
  • Participant profile:
    • Age: mainly 18-30 years
    • Gender: predominantly male
    • Sports: individual and team
  • Open-ended questions on perceived barriers, unmet needs, and attitudes toward psychological support

Quantitative Research

  • Online survey with 26 responses
  • Closed and open-ended questions
  • Objectives:
    • Measure mental health perceptions
    • Identify adoption barriers
    • Assess willingness to use a platform like Psycho Pro
