Lag-User Method

What is the Lag-User Method?

An approach that identifies late adopters of a technology or service to understand adoption barriers and develop innovative solutions.

Why are amateur athletes "late adopters"?

  • In sports culture, mental health issues are perceived as weaknesses
  • Priority given to physical performance over mental health
  • Stigma and denial leading to avoiding seeking help
  • Lack of services adapted to their specific needs

Source: Bayside Sports

"Psycho Pro aims to help athletes normalize mental health care in sports by removing these barriers."
