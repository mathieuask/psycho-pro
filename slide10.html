Conclusion

Project Summary

Psycho Pro aims to bridge a critical gap in mental health support for amateur athletes, by addressing the barriers identified through our research.

Our Lag-User method approach allowed us to identify the specific needs of amateur athletes, often overlooked in discussions about mental health in sports.

Expected Impact

Reduction of stigma around mental health in sports

Improved access to mental health professionals

Creation of a healthier and more balanced sports environment

Our Vision

Normalize mental health support in amateur and semi-professional sports, making mental health an integral part of athletes' training and development.

"Mental strength is just as important as physical strength."
