Results

Qualitative Findings

Semi-structured interviews with 10 amateur athletes (18-30 years)

Stigma and Denial

Psychological support perceived as weakness, especially in male sports.

"I postponed help despite clear signs of stress and declining performance."

Access and Availability

Difficulty finding qualified, specialized sports psychologists.

"Impossible to find a professional who truly understands my discipline."

Practical Barriers

Lack of time, high cost, uncertainty about confidentiality.

"I can't afford to consult regularly."
